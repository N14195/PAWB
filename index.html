About PAWB

PAWB Physiotherapy was established in 2022, and stands for Performance And Well-Being. Pawb is also the Welsh word for everyone/everybody, and we believe that elite level physiotherapy should be available to everyone, without the wait.

We understand the impact that pain or dysfunction can have on your life, whether that be long-standing arthritic knee pain, a debilitating episode of back pain, or an acute muscle tear. At PAWB we will accurately assess, diagnose, and create a bespoke treatment plan to help you achieve your specific goals, and get you back doing what you love most.

We appreciate that everyone’s goals will be different, whether its walking to the corner shop pain free, or preparing for your next ultra marathon. We take the time to understand what your requirements are and give you a range of potential treatment options tailored to your needs. This may involve hands on therapy, a bespoke rehabilitation programme to fit around your lifestyle, onward referral for diagnostic imaging, or to one of our trusted partners in another allied health profession.

We offer a range of services specific to your requirements – whether that be a one-off appointment, regular check-ins and updated plans, or home visits.

Get in touch to see how we can work with you, and start your journey with PAWB today.
